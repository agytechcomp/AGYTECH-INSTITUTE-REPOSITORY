## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Overview
Agytech Computer Training Institute is committed to providing high-quality education in computer application and information technology, with a special emphasis on empowering women and youths. Our programs are designed to bridge the digital divide and foster inclusivity in the tech industry.

### 1.2 Mission Statement
Our mission is to empower women and youths by providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the technology sector, thereby contributing to a more inclusive and diverse tech community.

---

## 2. Empowering Women and Youths Project

### 2.1 Project Overview
The "Empowering Women and Youths into the World of Technology" project aims to provide specialized training and resources to underrepresented groups, focusing on women and young people. This initiative seeks to enhance their technical skills, boost their confidence, and open up career opportunities in the tech industry.

### 2.2 Objectives
- **Increase Access:** Provide affordable and accessible technology education to women and youths.
- **Skill Development:** Offer comprehensive training in essential IT skills and advanced technologies.
- **Career Support:** Facilitate internships, job placements, and mentorship programs.
- **Community Engagement:** Foster a supportive community for networking and collaboration.

### 2.3 Impact Metrics
- Number of women and youths enrolled and graduated.
- Employment rates post-completion.
- Participant satisfaction and feedback.
- Community engagement levels and networking opportunities created.

### Target Audience

- Women of all ages seeking to improve their digital skills.
- Youths (ages 15-30) looking to enhance their employability in the tech sector.
- Underrepresented and economically disadvantaged groups in the community.

## 3. Course Catalog

### 3.1 Basic Computer skills
- **Introduction to Computers**
  - Course Code: ITC101
  - Duration: 4 weeks
  - Description: Basics of computer operation, MS Office Suite, Internet browsing.

- **Basic Programming**
  - Course Code: BP102
  - Duration: 6 weeks
  - Description: Introduction to programming using Python, basic algorithms, and problem-solving techniques.

### 3.2 Intermediate Courses
- **Web Development**
  - Course Code: WD201
  - Duration: 8 weeks
  - Description: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and introductory frameworks such as Bootstrap.

- **Database Management**
  - Course Code: DB202
  - Duration: 6 weeks
  - Description: Fundamentals of database design, SQL, and data manipulation.

### 3.3 Advanced Courses
- **Advanced Programming**
  - Course Code: AP301
  - Duration: 10 weeks
  - Description: Advanced concepts in Python and Java, including OOP, data structures, and algorithms.

- **Network Security**
  - Course Code: NS302
  - Duration: 8 weeks
  - Description: Principles of network security, cryptographic techniques, and secure communication protocols.

## 5. Admissions

### 5.1 Application Process
- **Step 1:** visit our Agytech Institution Office  for application letter.
- **Step 2:** Submit required documents (Contact information, identification, etc.).
- **Step 3:** Attend an interview (in-person ).
- **Step 4:** Receive admission after the interview.

### 5.2 Tuition and Fees
- **Basic Courses:** $200 per course
- **Intermediate Courses:** $300 per course
- **Advanced Courses:** $400 per course

### 5.3 Scholarships
Agytech offers a limited number of merit-based and need-based scholarships specifically for women and youths. Please contact the admissions office for more details.

## 6. Policies

### 6.1 Attendance Policy
Regular attendance is mandatory. Students must attend at least 80% of the classes to pass.
